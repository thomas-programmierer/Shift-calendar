Welcome to "Shift Calendar"

About this app

The shift calendar is an app to register your worker's shifts at your workplace. Like if "Jhon" will work from 9 AM - 12 PM you can register that on the app. This app can just have one shift at a time. Like at 10 AM its Jhon shift. So you can not add another shift.

How does this app works

This app uses your local storage to save data. The data is not saved on the server. The only one that can see the shifts is the one who has access to your computer.

To add a shift click on the button add a shift on top. And then register the name, description, hour, day and expend time. The description is not required.

The expend time is how many hours in additional someone will work. If you leave at zero it means that the shift will be just one hour. If you leave it at two it means the shift will be three hours.
And you can pick the color of your shift

About the code

This app/ webpage is made with pure Javascript and HTML, CSS. This app does not use any external libraries. Or any server-side technology.
The app fonts are from google fonts and icons are from font awsome
